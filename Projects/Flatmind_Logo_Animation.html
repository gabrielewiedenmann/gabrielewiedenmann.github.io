Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Gabriele, a Milan-based Communication Designer. Usually, I try to put myself out there and to approach situations with determination and positivity. I love coding and design websites, but my biggest passion is Motion Design. I think that motion can create magic and strong feelings more than any other stuff, and in my future I would like to work and create these. I'm a Cinema-addict, and during my free-time I love to go snowboarding and to discover places sorrounded by nature.
